Goals

Dentistry 

Becoming a Dentist takes “about 10 years… Its takes 4 years to obtain a bachelor’s degree, and then another 4 years of dental school.” When I first asked my parents for braces they weren’t really interested in getting them for me and one day I decided to go to work with my mom. One of her Co-workers had just got braces and started telling her about all the details, my mom then considered getting me braces and before I knew it I was going to the Dentist office that her Co-worker told her about 3 days later. I was so glad because I’ve been wanting braces for a while and this is when I decided I was interested in being a Dentist.

Orthodontist

Although I can stop at Dentistry I want to specialize and become an Orthodontist. Going into a profession like this requires 2 more years of school which is quite a long time but worth it when you get there. I’ve spoken to my Dentist about her experience and that really exposed me to what being a dentist is like and the process you have to go through; she is also taking her studies further to specialize in being an Orthodontist.

Create a Business

After I finish my post-doctoral program for the studies of orthodontics I want to create a business considering the fact that I want to be able to schedule my own hours. Managers also get to decide the best course of action out of two or more possible courses of action by marking morals to the expected benefits of different courses of action and comparing those values.

Freelance Make-up

I’ve had an interest in make-up ever since I was little. Like every little girl I would play in my mom’s make-up and of course ruin a lot of her things. As I started to get older, I started to have make-up of my own and I’d get some from my mom. Also, since I have a Job I buy some on my own. My knowledge on make-up grew when I’d watch YouTube videos. When I finish my studies of orthodontics I will continue to do make-up as a hobby.
