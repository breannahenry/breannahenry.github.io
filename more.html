About Me

               

     My main interest entering my highschool magnet program is technology and my excitement for technology all started with the program my current school now offers. When I first started to look into the programs my school I was stuck in between dance and technology production. When I started to think about which one I liked more I asked myself which one do I most likely see myself doing in the future? And there my answer was technology production was what I tried out for. When I saw that I had made it in I was glad.

    As I started to learn the programs being taught in this class I started to find that it was very interesting to me and I liked it a lot. I’ve challenged myself to learn something that I have never tried before and I saw that it wasn’t so bad after all. It affected me in the best way possible. A time that I have challenged myself to do something was when I had to perform in front of an entire audience and I was very nervous considering it being my first time. The way I got through it was by telling myself, what’s the worst that could happen?
    An accomplishment that has marked my transition from childhood to adulthood within my community is not caring what others think of me. For a long time I would question everything I do because I would worry about what the next person might be thinking. The way I got over that phase was not only maturity alone but through many friendships. I learned that in the end you only have yourself and you need to do what’s best for you.
    I used to do dance and that somewhat relieved my shy side, I can now be more confident when I meet new people. Dancing was a way for me to express my feelings through movement and it really did work. I once came off stage crying because of how much the song and the dancing touched me and my dance teacher was so surprised because she has never seen me dance like that before. I am very thankful for all the experiences that I have gone through because it has shaped me into the person I am today.
